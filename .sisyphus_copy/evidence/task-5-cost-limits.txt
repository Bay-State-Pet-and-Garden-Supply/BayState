================================================================================
CIRCUIT BREAKER BEHAVIOR
================================================================================

1. Each extraction that exceeds MAX_COST_PER_PAGE increments the strike counter
2. After CIRCUIT_BREAKER_THRESHOLD (3) consecutive overruns:
   - Circuit breaker activates for that scraper
   - All future check_cost_budget() calls return False
   - Logging shows: "Circuit breaker activated for scraper 'X' after 3 overruns"
3. Circuit breaker is per-scraper (isolated between scrapers)
4. Reset via reset_circuit_breaker(scraper_name) after manual intervention
5. Successful extraction (under limit) resets the counter for that scraper
